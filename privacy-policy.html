1Overview and Our Core Privacy Promise

The short version: Your images never leave your device. We do not see them, store them, or share them. All image processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No image data is ever transmitted to our servers.

Pixel Forge Stack is a collection of free, browser-based image editing tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website and use our tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using Pixel Forge Stack, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our website.

2Image Data and Processing

This is the most important section of this Privacy Policy for most of our users.

We never receive your images. When you upload an image to any Pixel Forge Stack tool, the image is loaded directly into your browser's memory using the browser's built-in File API. The image is then processed using the HTML5 Canvas API, which runs entirely within your browser on your own device. The processed image is then made available for you to download.

At no point in this process does the image data leave your device. No image file or image data is transmitted to our servers or to any third-party server. We have no technical ability to view, access, or store your images. Even if we wanted to, we could not — the architecture of our tools simply does not involve sending image data anywhere.

This means:

  • We do not store any of your images on our servers.
  • We do not have any record of what images you have uploaded or edited.
  • We cannot see the contents of your images.
  • Your images are completely safe from any data breach on our end, because we simply do not have them.
  • You can use our tools to edit sensitive or personal photos without any risk that those photos will end up on a third-party server.

3Usage Data and Analytics

While we do not collect your images, we do collect some basic, anonymous usage data about how our website is used. This helps us understand which tools are most popular, where our visitors come from, and how we can improve the website.

The usage data we collect may include:

  • The pages you visit on our website and the time you spend on them.
  • The type of browser and operating system you are using.
  • Your approximate geographic location based on your IP address (country or city level only — we do not track your precise location).
  • How you arrived at our website (for example, from a search engine or another website).
  • General information about how you interact with our tools (for example, which tools you use), but never any information about the content of the images you process.

This data is collected and processed in an anonymised and aggregated form. It is not linked to any personally identifiable information. We use this data only to understand and improve our website.

4Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. Pixel Forge Stack uses a minimal set of cookies.

Essential cookies are cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information and cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use our website. We use anonymous analytics to track page views and tool usage in aggregate. These cookies do not identify you personally.

Advertising cookies may be set by our advertising partners to show you relevant advertisements. See the Advertising section below for more details.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website. Most modern browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified before a cookie is set.

5Advertising

Pixel Forge Stack is a free service supported by advertising. We may display advertisements on our website from third-party advertising networks. These advertising partners may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to show you advertisements that are relevant to your interests.

We do not share your personal information with our advertising partners. Any data that advertising networks collect about you is governed by their own privacy policies, not ours. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

You can opt out of interest-based advertising through the settings in your browser, or by using tools such as the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out tool or the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.

If you prefer not to see personalised advertising, you can also use a browser extension that blocks advertisements.

6Third-Party Services

Pixel Forge Stack uses a small number of third-party services to help run the website. These include:

  • Google Fonts — we use Google Fonts to load the Inter typeface used throughout the website. When your browser loads a Google Font, a request is made to Google's servers. Google's Privacy Policy applies to any data collected in this process.
  • Analytics services — we use an analytics service to understand how our website is used. This service receives anonymised usage data as described in the Usage Data section above.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) — some of our tools load JavaScript libraries from CDN providers. When your browser loads these scripts, a request is made to the CDN's servers.

We carefully select third-party services that respect user privacy and comply with applicable privacy laws. However, once you interact with a third-party service, their own privacy policies govern how they handle your data.

7Children's Privacy

Pixel Forge Stack is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

If you are under 18 years of age, please use this website only with the involvement and consent of a parent or guardian.

8Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These may include:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request that we correct any inaccurate personal data.
  • The right to request that we delete your personal data.
  • The right to object to or restrict our processing of your personal data.
  • The right to data portability.

Because we collect only minimal, anonymised usage data and do not collect any image data or personally identifiable information, most of these rights are straightforward to exercise. Since we do not store images or personal accounts, there is very little personal data for you to access, correct, or delete.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact section below.

9Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, the tools we offer, or applicable laws and regulations. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. If we make significant changes, we may also provide a more prominent notice on our website.

Your continued use of Pixel Forge Stack after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please do not hesitate to contact us. We take all privacy-related inquiries seriously and will respond as promptly as possible.

You can reach us by visiting our website and using the contact form, or by sending an email to the address listed on our website. When contacting us about a privacy matter, please include as much detail as possible so that we can address your concern effectively.

We are committed to working with you to resolve any privacy concerns you may have. If you are not satisfied with our response, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in your country.
