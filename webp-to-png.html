WEBP vs PNG

What Is WEBP and Why Convert It to PNG?

WEBP is a modern image format developed by Google. It produces smaller file sizes than JPG and PNG while maintaining good image quality, which makes it excellent for websites. However, WEBP has a significant disadvantage — it is not supported by all applications and devices.

Older software like Microsoft Office, many image editing programs, and some older mobile apps cannot open WEBP files. If you have downloaded a WEBP image from the web and cannot open it on your device, or if you need to insert it into a document or presentation and it is not working, converting it to PNG solves the problem instantly.

PNG is a universally supported format. Every operating system, every browser, every image editing app, and virtually every device in the world can open a PNG file without any issues. PNG also supports transparent backgrounds, making it perfect for logos, icons, and graphics that need to sit on top of other content.

Our WEBP to PNG converter works entirely in your browser. You upload the WEBP file, the tool converts it using the Canvas API on your device, and you download the PNG. The whole process takes just a second and nothing is ever sent to a server.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does converting WEBP to PNG lose quality? ▾
PNG is a lossless format, meaning the conversion does not reduce image quality. The PNG you download will look identical to the original WEBP. In fact, the PNG file may be slightly larger than the WEBP, because PNG does not use the same aggressive compression that WEBP does. That is the trade-off — PNG is more compatible but has a larger file size.
Will the transparent background be preserved? ▾
Yes. If your WEBP image has a transparent background, the PNG output will also have a transparent background. This is one of the key advantages of converting to PNG rather than JPG, since JPG does not support transparency.
Can I convert JPG or other formats to PNG too? ▾
Yes. While this tool is designed for WEBP to PNG conversion, it also accepts JPG, GIF, BMP, and other image formats and converts them to PNG. Just upload any image file and click the download button.
