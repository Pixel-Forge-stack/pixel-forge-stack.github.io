Why Convert Images to PDF

When Is It Useful to Convert Images to PDF?

There are many everyday situations where converting an image to PDF is the best option. Here are the most common reasons people use this tool.

Sharing documents professionally. PDF is the standard format for professional documents. If you have scanned a document, receipt, or certificate as a photo, converting it to PDF makes it look more official and ensures it is easy to open on any device.

Combining multiple images into one file. Instead of sending ten separate image files, you can combine them into a single PDF. This makes it easier to share, easier for the recipient to view in order, and easier to print.

Submitting documents online. Many government portals, banks, universities, and companies require documents to be submitted as PDF files. If you have photos of your documents, our tool converts them to PDF in seconds.

Creating simple photo albums or portfolios. You can arrange multiple photos in order, convert them to a single PDF, and share that PDF as a simple photo book or portfolio. Perfect for sharing memories with family or showing your work to a client.

Preserving quality. Images in PDF format are generally better preserved than images sent through messaging apps, which often compress and reduce the quality of photos. Sending a PDF keeps the image quality exactly as it was.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I convert multiple images to one PDF? ▾
Yes. You can upload as many images as you want and they will all be combined into a single PDF file. Each image will appear on its own page in the PDF, in the order you uploaded them.
Is my image uploaded to a server? ▾
No. Your images never leave your device. The PDF is generated entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. This makes the process completely private and secure.
What image formats are supported? ▾
You can upload JPG, PNG, and WEBP images. All of these will be embedded correctly in the output PDF file.
What page size should I choose? ▾
For most documents and printing, A4 is the best choice as it is the standard paper size in most countries. If you are in the United States, Letter is the standard. If you want the PDF page to be exactly the same size as your image, choose "Fit to Image".
Can I reorder the images before converting? ▾
The images are placed in the PDF in the order they were uploaded. To change the order, simply remove an image using the X button and re-upload it in the position you want it to appear.
